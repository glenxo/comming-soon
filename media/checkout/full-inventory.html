More about this trip

Chame is a small town located on the Pacific coast of Panama, in Central America. Nestled between the Pacific Ocean and the majestic mountains of the Cordillera Central,this town is known for its stunning natural beauty, abundant wildlife, and rich history.
Located just a short drive from Panama City, Los Cajones de Chame is a popular destination for tourists and locals alike. Visitors to this charming town can enjoy a wide range of activities including Beach activities. Chame is known for its beautiful beaches,
such as Playa Chame, Playa Blanca and Playa El Arenal , where you can swim, sunbathe,
and participate in water sports like jet skiing, paddle boarding, and parasailing. Surfing: Chame is known as a popular spot for surfers, particularly at the breaks of Punta Chame,
El Palmar and Venao. Fishing: The town of Chame has a small but active fishing fleet,
and visitors can take a fishing trip out to the Pacific Ocean to catch a variety of fish such as tuna,
marlin, and sailfish. Hiking and Biking: Chame offers many different trails for hiking and biking,
ranging from easy to strenuous. Some popular trails include the Las Uvas mountain range and
El Valle de Antón, which is a valley located near Chame. Scuba Diving: Chame is a perfect location for diving with its crystal clear waters,
and diverse marine life.
Horseback Riding: Visitors can explore the rural areas around Chame on horseback.
Panamanian Culture and History: Chame is home to many historical and cultural sites,
such as the Chame Cultural Center and the Chame Church. Visitors can also explore the local markets and shops to learn about the traditional crafts and goods of the region.
Relaxation: Visitors can also enjoy relaxing on the beach, laying on hammocks,
enjoying local and international foods, or just relaxing in a comfortable cabin or hotel.
One of the most popular attractions in Chame is the Cajones de Chame National Park. This protected area is home to a diverse array of plant and animal species, ncluding monkeys, sloths, iguanas, and over 300 species of birds. Visitors can explore the park on foot or by bike, taking in the breathtaking views of the mountains and the ocean as they go. In addition to its natural beauty. Los Cajones de Chame is also home to a rich cultural heritage. The town has a long history dating back to the pre-Columbian era,and its residents are proud of their indigenous roots. Visitors can learn about the town's history and culture by visiting the local museums and cultural centers, or by chatting with the friendly locals. Despite its small size, Los Cajones de Chame has a thriving tourism industry,with a range of accommodation options available to suit all budgets. From luxury hotels and resorts to cozy bed and breakfasts,there is something for everyone in this charming town. Overall, Los Cajones de Chame is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Panama. relaxaion, or a chance to immerse yourself in local culture, this charming town has something to offer. So why wait? Pack your bags and head to Los Cajones de Chame today!
